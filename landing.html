Fire Emblem Primer

Fire Emblem is a video game series created by the Japanese company Intelligent Systems. Intelligent Systems is a company very closely affilated with Nintendo another Japanese game company. Fire Emblem is a tactical wargame rpg. In it players control many unique units to fight a war. Combat is conducted by a comparison of each units stats and weapons. Random chance is then created from that comparison that allow clutch moments and sudden disasters. Units can experience death in Fire Emblem. A unit that loses all its hit points(HP) is removed from the game and unusable afterwards.

Fire Emblem first began in 1990 and has since gone on to release fifteen titles and three spin offs. This website will give a brief overview of Fire Emblem Games to help appreciate the progress of time for this series.
